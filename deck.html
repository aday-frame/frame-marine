02 The problem
A 48-metre yacht has 12 crew, 300 maintenance items, 4 charter bookings a year, and 47 compliance certificates.
It's managed on WhatsApp.
Problem 1
Everything lives in the captain's head
No handover system. When a captain leaves, institutional knowledge walks out with them. Owners have no visibility.
Problem 2
Compliance is managed in spreadsheets
Certificates expire without warning. STCW hours tracked manually. A flag inspection finds gaps nobody knew existed.
Problem 3
Owner experience is inconsistent
Preferences re-entered every visit. Charter guests get generic service. No system ensures the vessel always meets standard.
