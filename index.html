Frame Marine connects deck operations, engineering, compliance, charter, and owner reporting in a single system designed for how yachts actually work.

01
Captain-first operations
Start every day with the Captain's Brief — a single screen that surfaces what's urgent, what's today, and what needs your attention before you leave the wheelhouse. Work orders, monitoring alerts, crew status, and the day's schedule at a glance.

Track certificates for vessel and crew, log STCW hours of rest, manage ISM documentation, and run safety drills — all with expiry alerts so nothing sneaks up on you at a port state control inspection.
