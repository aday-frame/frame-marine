Owner mode
Mr. Smith is aboard or arriving.

Charter mode
A paying guest has booked the vessel.
Their profile drives the experience for this booking. Preferences are captured and retained for repeat charters.

Primary object: the booking.
Ops mode
No guest aboard. Vessel is in maintenance, yard, or transit.
Operations are the primary view — WOs, PMS, monitoring, provisioning.

Primary object: the vessel.
